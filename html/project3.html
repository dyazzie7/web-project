Welcome page

My work has a Healthy Competition every year. There are two categories: a group category and individual category. The wieght lost is based on body fat lost.

The group category consists of four team members. Two males and two females. Each member gets weighed in, our height is measured and then they use a body fat analyzer. This is a six-week challenge, so we encouraged each other to lose 3 to 5 lbs. each.

The same information is used in the individual challenge as well, if a team member decides to participate in both challenges. The individual challenge is a four-month challenge.

I have joined the individual challenge for two years, and each year I have gained weight, haha. This year I joined a team hoping that they well inspire me more, and because I do not want to let them down by gaining weight. We are put up against other teams that joined the company. Whoever loses the most weight gets a cash prize. Not a lot but it is something.

I think I am doing good this year with this challenge. I replaced my breakfast with protein shakes, cutting out sugar, and bread. Of course, cutting back on my serving size and counting my carbs and taking walks when I can, I also bought a small walking treadmill. I do slip sometimes, especially on weekends. Hopefully I will make the 5 lb. mark.

I do not think we well win though because my team members seem to still be eating what they want. One of them will buy and bring food back into the office making me want to break my goal and get what she is eating. I started leaving my phone and wallet in my vehicle.

Strategy for Weight Loss

  • Count carbs
  • Go on daily walks
  • No more candy
  • No more bread
  • More protein
  • Include the kids to make the walk interesting!
